![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/deskjet-3700-.jpg)

**3)** Click**Software, Drivers and Firmware** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/deskjet-3700-driver.jpg)
