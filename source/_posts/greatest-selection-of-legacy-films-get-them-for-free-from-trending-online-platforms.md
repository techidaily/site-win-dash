## How to Remove Audio from a Video on Mac?

iMovie is a free video editing application designed for Mac users with little or no video editing experience. **Step 1.** Open iMovie and click ‚ÄúProjects‚Äù > ‚ÄúCreate New‚Äù to create a new project.  
**Step 2.** Click the down arrow icon (or press ‚ÄúCommand + I‚Äù) to import a video from your computer. Next, drag the video to the timeline.
